About me

Hello and welcome to my page, I was born and raised in Kansas City.

My parents, both immigrants from Laos came here to the United States looking for better opportunity.

I basically grew up outside, always playing some sport or just wandering off into the woods; absolutely hated being inside.

As I got older and matured, I started to figure out what hobbies I truly enjoyed and things that brought me joy-

mechanics, technology and

fabrication; combined! I then started working on automobiles and motorcycles, tearing them down and rebuilding them

from the ground up as well

as dissecting the inner workings of their brains/computers that ran them. All very fascinating to me and to this day I am still so intruiged by all of it.

My thoughts are that someday I will be able to create something whether it be a vehicle or some sort of automated machine that will somehow benefit

our ever evolving world.
